Welcome to the National University of Computer Studies

The National University of Computer Studies (NUCS) stands at the forefront of technology education and research in Myanmar. Established with a vision to empower the next generation of innovators, NUCS offers a dynamic learning environment where students are encouraged to explore, create, and lead in the ever-evolving field of computer science.

Our university provides a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills. With a dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, and strong industry partnerships, NUCS ensures that graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of the global digital economy.

At NUCS, we believe in fostering a culture of curiosity, collaboration, and continuous learning. Our vibrant campus life, diverse student community, and commitment to academic excellence make NUCS a premier destination for aspiring computer professionals. Join us and become part of a community that is shaping the future of technology in Myanmar and beyond.
